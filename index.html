Importance of IT

Internet and information technology play a very important role in every sector of businesses. Nowadays business is not limited in a brick and motor traditional store. The businesses are expanding in online store as well as the operation procedure also changing accordingly. In logistics and supply chain operation technology has a significant used, for example; forecast of demand, procurement process, supply of inventory, supplier relationship management, customer relationship management, data sharing, inventory management, etc. Day by day the use of technology is increasing. So, compete in the current job market, it is crucial important to have competent and current up to date knowledge of IT. Slowly the use of manual order like, processing, invoicing, quotation are eliminating and IT is taking this place and all the task is perform online through internet. So, in near future there will be no alternative of IT.

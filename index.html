Importance of IT

 

Internet and information technology play a very important role in every sector of businesses. Nowadays business is not limited in a brick and motor traditional store. The businesses are expanding in online store as well as the operation procedure also changing accordingly. In logistics and supply chain operation technology has a significant used, for example; forecast of demand, procurement process, supply of inventory, supplier relationship management, customer relationship management, data sharing, inventory management, etc.  Day by day the use of technology is increasing.
